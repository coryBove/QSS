Getting Started

How does this work? How do I get the ball rolling? It’s really quite simple and best of all – it’s FREE! We’ll email you a spreadsheet listing a number of select functions/features. You simply rate & weight each one using a numerical scale. Then, provide some additional information on your company and needs in the spreadsheet as well. Save the spreadsheet and email it back to us. We will then run the algorithm and do some additional research for you. Finally, we’ll reply with the results – some select systems/software vendor(s) for you to contact. We do not consider the price of the software. There are companies out there that offer free pricing guides for various software products. Those pricing guides are obsolete the day they are created. Also, pricing models & tactics change more often than the weather. In general, think of this like a HR function narrowing down 100 job applicants to a chosen few for you to interview.

To Get Started:

  1. Send an email request to QualitySystemsSelect@gmail.com and state in the Subject line: "Quality Systems Matching"
  2. Be sure and include your name, company name, address, and contact phone number.
  3. Feel free to list any unique or specific requirements for your system/software.
  4. We will reply with our needs assessment spreadsheet for you to complete and send back to us.

That's it! We look forward to hearing from you!
